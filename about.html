The Engineer

Madeline Guzman

"I'm an audio engineer and sound designer with a foundation built in two demanding schools — the U.S. Marine Corps and Full Sail University. After 9.5 years of active duty service as a Field Radio Operator, I brought the same technical precision and high-pressure reliability I developed in the field into the studio. I hold a BS in Audio Production and certifications in Pro Tools, Wwise 101, and FMOD, and I work across game audio implementation, music mixing, and vocal production. I'm currently playtesting professionally while actively pursuing a career creating the audio worlds I've loved since childhood."
